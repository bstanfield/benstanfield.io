Drawdown for Dummies

My attempt to summarize the best solutions to climate change, as described in the book Drawdown.

Sun Jan 19 2025


The Detroit Dam in Detroit, Oregon uses gravity to generate renewable, hydroelectric power.

It took me over a year and a half of working on Wren to finally sit down and read Drawdown, a seminal text on climate solutions. Despite all of the websites and companies that cite the book's solutions, most people chaffe at actually sitting down and reading the whole thing, front to back. And rightfully so, as the book is more akin to a textbook than a novel.
So, I decided to make the experience more fun for myself by boiling down some of the solutions into a few sentences. I hope this helps you get excited about a particular solution, so much so that you purchase Drawdown and give reading it a try!
